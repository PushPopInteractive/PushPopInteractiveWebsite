Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026 · Applies to all PushPop Interactive apps, including Orbcrash

The short version

PushPop Interactive games do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. None at all.

No tracking, no accounts, no ads

Our apps have no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. They do not connect to the internet, so nothing you do in our apps ever leaves your device.

Your game progress

Your game progress is saved only in local storage on your own device. If you delete an app, that data is removed with it.

Nothing to request or delete

Because we collect no data, there is nothing to request, export, delete, or opt out of.

Changes to this policy

If a future game ever works differently, we'll update this page and say so plainly before it ships.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at 📬 pushpopinteractive@gmail.com
