Frequently asked

Is Orbcrash out yet?

Not quite — Orbcrash is coming soon to the App Store. Email us if you'd like to be notified when it launches.

How is my game progress saved?

Progress is saved locally on your device. It isn't uploaded anywhere, so it stays private to you. Deleting the app removes it.

Does Orbcrash need an internet connection?

No. Orbcrash plays fully offline — no accounts, no ads, no connection required.

What is the ORBFALL?

It's the ultimate move. Fill the ORBFALL meter during a run and unleash a board-sweeping cascade for a massive chain of bursts.

I found a bug — what should I include?

Let us know what happened, what you expected, and your device model and iOS version. A screenshot or screen recording helps a lot.
