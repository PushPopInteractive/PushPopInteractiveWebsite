Frequently asked

How is my game progress saved?

Progress is saved locally on your device. It isn't uploaded anywhere, so it stays private to you. Deleting the app removes it.

Do your games need an internet connection?

No. PushPop games play fully offline — no accounts, no ads, no connection required.

When is [game] coming out?

Orbcrash is coming soon to the App Store, and the rest of the catalog is in active development. Email us if you'd like a heads-up when a specific game launches.

I found a bug — what should I include?

Let us know what happened, what you expected, which game and your device model and iOS version. A screenshot or screen recording helps a lot.

Can I request a feature?

Absolutely. Some of our favorite ideas came from players. No promises — but every suggestion gets read.
