Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

The short version

Orbcrash does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. None at all.

No tracking, no accounts, no ads

The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. It does not connect to the internet, so nothing you do in the app ever leaves your device.

Your game progress

Your game progress is saved only in local storage on your own device. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it.

Nothing to request or delete

Because we collect no data, there is nothing to request, export, delete, or opt out of.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at 📬 pushpopinteractive@gmail.com
