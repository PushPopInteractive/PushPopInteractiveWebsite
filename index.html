The studio

Small studio. Big juice.

PushPop Interactive is what happens when one person refuses to stop making games. Every title on this page — the design, the code, the art direction, the sound of an orb going splorch — comes from a single very stubborn, very caffeinated human.

The house style: games that feel tactile and generous. Screens you want to touch, systems that reward one more run, and zero of the stuff that makes mobile games feel gross. Our games ship with no ads, no accounts, and no tracking — your progress lives on your device and nowhere else.

Built with ❤️ (and Godot) for iPhone and iPad.
