What is Orbcrash?

A bright, bursty puzzle game with a roguelike twist — every run is different, every match is satisfying.

🫧

Merge & Burst

Slide jelly orbs together to pop them. Bigger groups mean juicier, more satisfying bursts.

⚡️

Chain Reactions

One burst sets off the next. Build huge combos and watch the board erupt.

✨

Collect Charms

Pick up game-changing Charms between rounds and stack them into wild, run-defining combos.

🌈

Unleash ORBFALL

Fill the meter and trigger the ultimate — a cascade that sweeps the whole board.

🎲

Roguelike Runs

Surges, rounds, swaps and shops. No two runs play the same — push your luck and see how far you go.

📵

Play Anywhere

No accounts, no ads, no internet required. Just open it and play. Your progress stays on your device.
